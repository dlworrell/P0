# AES

AES is the Atarix Engineering Standard.

This repository is the canonical engineering record for the Catalyst and Atarix ecosystem. It contains the engineering creed, standards, decision records, research notes, case studies, and templates that guide how projects are designed, documented, reviewed, and maintained.

## Purpose

AES exists to preserve engineering knowledge so future maintainers can understand what was built, why it was built, and how it should evolve.

## Pillar I

First, Observe. Then, Understand. Then, Improve.

Observation precedes understanding. Understanding precedes improvement.

## Repository Role

- Just-a-Geek-LLC defines organizational governance.
- AES defines engineering governance.
- repo_templates provides reusable project scaffolding.
- AEMS, Atarix, JAG, and future projects inherit from AES.

## Current Structure

- creed: foundational philosophy
- standards: engineering standards and practices
- adr: architecture decision records
- research: investigations and proposed methods
- case-studies: operational lessons
- templates: document and rule templates
- references: external influences and source material

## Current Status

AES is in early foundation work. The initial objective is to capture the engineering philosophy, decision-making process, and documentation structure before substantial implementation begins in dependent projects.

## Core Idea

Technology is temporary. Engineering knowledge endures.
